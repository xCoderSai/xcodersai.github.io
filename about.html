ABOUT US!

We were founded in 2021 by CatieSai after she got jealous that SHE wasn’t in
a Hacktivism Group so she made her own! Most likely the first women-only, nonbinary-only Hacker Group, we don’t do any hardcore
pen testing. Instead, out goals include promoting mental wellness, disability in tech and women/nonbinary in tech.
